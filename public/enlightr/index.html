Everything you need to build fast, modern websites with Astro

Astro helps you build ultra-fast websites with zero JavaScript by default making performance the default, not an afterthought. Whether you're just starting out or migrating from another stack, the learning curve is gentle and the tooling is refreshingly simple.

You get full freedom to work with your favorite frameworks — React, Vue, Svelte, Solid — all in the same project. Combine them effortlessly while keeping your pages fast and clean. Write content in Markdown or MDX and enhance it with interactive components only where you need them.

Want to connect to a headless CMS or fetch data from an API? Astro lets you do it at build time or runtime, with full control. When it's time to go live, deploy seamlessly to Netlify, Vercel, or your own infrastructure — optimized and ready.

From performance-focused builds to smooth developer workflows, Astro keeps things productive. Enjoy fast reloads, great error messages, and a polished experience from start to ship — so you can launch confidently and showcase your work with pride.
